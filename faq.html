frequently asked questions

FAQ

Can I reserve a seat?
Yes, we accept reservations by phone.
- HONOKA COFFEE Tomizawa (022-743-3366)
Are pets allowed?
Only at HONOKA COFFEE Tomizawa. Sorry for any innconvience
Do you offer coffee brewing classes?
Classes are held 1~2 times a month at each store. (Currently held online) Please check the SNS of each store for details.
Is there a parking lot?
Yes! HONOKA COFFEE Tomizawa Main Store: 4 spaces
Where can I find information about store events?
You can check it on the official Instagram account. HONOKA COFFEE @honokacoffee.inc
Can I take coffee and sweets home?
Yes, it is available at each store.
Is it possible to give coffee as a gift?
Yes, we can tailor it to your budget, so please feel free to contact our staff.
If the quantity is large, it will take some time, so it will be smoother if you make a reservation in advance.
Is it possible to have coffee beans shipped from the store?
Yes, we ship nationwide. (Excluding Okinawa and remote islands)
For international shipping please ask a member of staff.
